Stay Cool and Comfortable with the Aeric Portable Air Conditioner

This versatile Aeric portable air conditioner delivers efficient cooling with a 5000 BTU capacity, meeting SACC standards and matching the performance of an 8000 BTU ASHRAE unit. Ideal for small to medium-sized rooms, it provides a comfortable environment even in the hottest weather.

Dual Functionality for Maximum Comfort

Equipped with a built-in 63-pint dehumidifier, this unit not only cools but also removes excess moisture from the air, making it perfect for maintaining a pleasant indoor climate. Whether you're dealing with humidity or heat, this device handles both with ease.

User-Friendly Controls and Setup

Enjoy hassle-free operation with the LED display and included remote control, allowing you to adjust settings from anywhere in the room. The 47.2-inch exhaust hose and bracket make installation simple, while the caster wheels ensure effortless mobility when you need to move it to a different location.

Designed for Versatile Use

This cooling dehumidifier is ideal for bedrooms, offices, living rooms, or any other space that needs year-round climate control. With a 400 sq. ft. coverage, it's capable of handling a wide range of room sizes, ensuring everyone stays cool and comfortable no matter where they are.

Quiet Operation, No Distractions

Operating at just 50 dB, this quiet portable AC runs smoothly without interrupting your peace. It's perfect for homes, offices, or any environment where noise levels need to be kept to a minimum.

Enhanced Air Quality

Features a washable filter that captures dust, pollen, and allergens, improving indoor air quality while also reducing the need for frequent maintenance. The timer function allows for energy-efficient operation, helping you save on utility bills without compromising comfort.

A Reliable Cooling Solution

Whether you're looking for a portable air conditioner, a dehumidifier air conditioner, or a cooling dehumidifier, the Aeric is an excellent choice. It combines efficiency, convenience, and comfort into one compact unit, making it a great investment for any home or workspace.
