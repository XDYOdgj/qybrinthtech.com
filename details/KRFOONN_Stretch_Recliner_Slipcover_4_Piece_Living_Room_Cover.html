KRFOONN 4-Piece Stretch Recliner Slipcover

Transform your recliner into a fresh and stylish piece of furniture with this versatile 4-piece slipcover. Designed for comfort, durability, and ease of use, it's the perfect way to update your living room without compromising on function.

Key Features

  • Soft and durable polyester and spandex blend for a snug, comfortable fit
  • Includes 2 arm pieces, 1 seat back piece, and 1 cover back piece for complete coverage
  • Built-in storage pocket for remote controls, magazines, or other essentials
  • Easy installation with clear instructions and labeled panels
  • Machine-washable design for simple cleaning and long-lasting use

Why Choose This Slipcover?

Whether you're lounging at home or entertaining guests, this slipcover adds both functionality and flair to your recliner chair. Its stretchy fabric conforms to the shape of your furniture without causing damage, ensuring a personalized fit every time. With multiple color options, it's easy to match your home decor and create a welcoming space that reflects your style.

Perfect For

Ideal for standard recliner chairs with seat widths of 30-40 inches and lengths of 23-35 inches. It's not only a great way to freshen up your furniture but also a practical solution for protecting it from daily use and wear.
