CRFATOP Large Ottoman Chair Slipcover – Durable and Stylish Protection

This printed chair slipcover is made from a blend of 92% polyester and 8% spandex, combining softness with stretch that makes it ideal for everyday use. The two-way stretch design ensures a perfect fit on most rectangular and 5-sided ottomans, while the sewn-in elastic band keeps it securely in place and prevents slipping. Whether you're looking to protect your furniture or add a modern touch to your space, this slipcover is a reliable and practical choice for any living room or dining area.

Key Features

  • Durable and comfortable fabric with 92% polyester and 8% spandex
  • Two-way stretch for a snug fit on most rectangular and 5-sided ottomans
  • Sewn-in elastic band to keep the slipcover in place and prevent slipping
  • Anti-pilling, wrinkle-resistant, and easy to maintain
  • Fashionable printed pattern that enhances your home decor

Why Choose This Product?

Your furniture deserves both protection and style. The CRFATOP printed chair slipcover is designed for easy installation and long-lasting use, making it perfect for homes with kids or pets. It not only guards your surfaces against spills and wear but also introduces a fresh, contemporary look that complements your interior design. A universal fit ensures that it works with ottomans measuring 27.56–37.43 inches in length, 19.69–27.56 inches in width, and 14.96–17.72 inches in height.

With machine-washable care and a sleek design, this slipcover is a must-have for any modern living space or casual dining area. Let your furniture shine with this stylish and functional upgrade.
