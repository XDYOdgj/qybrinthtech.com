GAOMON 8000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

Designed for comfort and convenience, this GAOMON portable air conditioner is perfect for cooling small to medium-sized rooms like your bedroom, home office, or apartment. With a cooling capacity of 8000 BTU, it efficiently maintains ideal temperatures even in larger spaces up to 350 square feet.

Whether you're cooling a small office or a family room, this unit delivers reliable performance without the need for permanent installation.
