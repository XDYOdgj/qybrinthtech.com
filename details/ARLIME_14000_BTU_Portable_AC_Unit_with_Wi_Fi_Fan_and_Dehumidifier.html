ARLIME 14000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

Get powerful, efficient cooling in any room with the ARLIME 14000 BTU portable air conditioner. This advanced unit is built to deliver cool comfort while keeping your energy bills in check thanks to its energy-saving inverter technology and ECO mode. With the ability to reduce electricity use by up to 45%, it's a smart and cost-effective choice for everyday use. The inverter system ensures precise temperature control, keeping your space comfortable without unnecessary energy waste.

The 3-in-1 functionality lets you switch between cooling, dehumidifying, and fan modes, making it adaptable to different weather conditions and room needs. Whether it's a sweltering summer day or a humid evening, this versatile AC unit provides the right setting for your comfort. Its quiet operation, with noise levels under 42dB, makes it perfect for bedrooms, offices, and other spaces where you want peace and quiet.

Compatible with Alexa and Google Assistant, you can control it remotely or with your voice for added ease and flexibility.
